Design Decisions

The primary audience for this website consists of young scholars interested in nomadic societies, Kazakh history, and the broader Eurasian region. The main purpose of this website is to clearly present my research question and provide a way for interested readers to connect.

The website is organized into three main sections: context, methodology, and research questions. The context section introduces Kazakh society, the Semirechye region, and the nomadic system. The methodology section explains the sources, variables, and research methods. The research questions section presents the main questions, research pathways, and limitations.
